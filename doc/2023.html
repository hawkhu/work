LIHTC Projects Situation in 2023:

At the end of 2023, 147 projects will expire among the Los Angeles County, including the expired projects between 2020 to 2022.

In the Greater East LA area, there are totally 10 expiring projects between 2020 to 2023. 2 of them is in East Los Angeles, 1 is in Alhambra, and 7 of them are in the East part of Los Angeles City.

In the Greater South LA area, there are totally 21 expiring projects between 2020 and 2023. 4 expiring projects are in Willowbrook, 2 of them are in Inglewood and 15 projects are in South part of Los Angeles City.
