Recommendation:

In our recommendation model, we included relevant variables in 4 categories: demography, housing condition, expiring LIHTC projects and opportunity zone assessment. In demography category, we used factors representing race, age, education attainment, poverty rate, unemployment rate, etc. In housing condition category, we took house value, rent, renter ratio, living space, commute time into our consideration. Also used in the model are the results of expiring projects and opportunity zone assessment mentioned before in every census tract in Los Angeles County. We simply assigned weight towards these four categories as the ratio 1:1:2:2, considering the current situation of affordable housing projects and the opportunity zone assessment from HUD may play important roles in decision-making process. Then we summed up the multiplication the weights and utilities of each factor in each census tract. This map shows final results of our recommendation model.

In this map, we ranked all the final results of each census tract. In the Greater South LA, there are 3 census tracts ranking in top 10% and 24 census tracts ranking in top 10-25%. In the Greater East LA, we also have 10 census tracts ranking in top 10-25%.

Therefore, our first recommendation is these three census tracts in the south part of Los Angeles City: Census Tract 2264.10, Census Tract 2264.20 and Census Tract 2431.
