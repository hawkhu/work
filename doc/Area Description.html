Area description:

There are 475 affordable housing projects serving in Los Angeles County currently, based on LIHTC dataset by HUD.

In the Greater East LA area, there are 34 affordable housing projects serving.  Each of Monterey Park and Alhambra has 1 project currently serving, and the East Los Angeles has 8. The East part of Los Angeles City has the rest 24 projects. Commerce and Montebello don’t have any affordable housing project taking effect.

In the Greater South LA area, there are 76 projects currently serving. Each of Florence-Graham, Westmont and West Athens has 1 project taking effect. Inglewood has 8 projects, Willowbrook has 9, and the South part of Los Angeles City has 56 projects serving currently. There is no affordable housing project serving in Ladera Heights and View Park-Windsor Hills.
