Opportunity Zone:

HUD has assessed the selected some potential census tracts as opportunity zones in 2017. Businesses, equipment, and real property in opportunity zones can receive tax breaks on capital gains to incentivize investment.  There are 127 opportunity zones in Los Angeles County.

There are 10 census tracts selected as opportunity zone in the Greater East LA. 1 of them is in Commerce, 2 in East Los Angeles, and 7 of them are in the east part of Los Angeles City.

There are 28 census tracts selected as opportunity zone in the Greater South LA. Each of Inglewood, Willowbrook and West Athens has 1 in their area. Westmont has 2 opportunity zones, and the south part of Los Angeles City has 23 opportunity zones.
